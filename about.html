I was born and raised in France, then lived in NYC for 4 years before moving to the SF Bay Area. I spent almost two decades in the digital industry working for major web studios and startups. I am currently Product Designer at Meta in California.

I am a generalist focused on craft, driven by quality and purpose. I have expertise in leading large scale projects, from Product Strategy, User Experience & Interaction Design. Convinced that creativity happens across disciplines, I often reconnect with my background in Computer Science and Human-Computer Interaction.

In my younger days, I started coding on a C64 and discovered the possibilities offered by screen interfaces. I was then inspired to explore the different ways I could interact with this new medium. Today, I contribute by bringing technology into people's lives through simple and elegant digital products.
